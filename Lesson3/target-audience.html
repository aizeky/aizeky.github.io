Target Audience

The Target audience of this website are people who would like easy access to information about the weather. The website, Dodge the Cold, is for people who want to be prepared for extreme cold weathers. The website will give suggestions on what they should prepare for according to weather. It will suggest that how warm they should dress up for the weather, or just stay at home and be lazy.

Personas

1st Persona

The first person could be a company manager that works from home due to the pandemic. This worker plans to schedule a meeting with his or her team the next day. However, because of their location and also because of the winter, the manager is trying to decide whether it is best to meet in person or do the meeting through zoom. DodgeTheCold.com is a great source of information to help with that decision.
